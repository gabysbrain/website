Computer scientist with extensive experience building systems from the ground up as well as research in ML/AI with a focus on visual analysis in multi-dimensional continuous spaces and understanding complex models. I also dabble in functional programming. I love learning about new technologies and how I can use them to create better software more easily. I am particularly a fan of the Haskell and PureScript languages.
